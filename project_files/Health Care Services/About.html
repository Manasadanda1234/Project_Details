Medical Services:-

Hospitalists

A hospitalist is a doctor who specializes in treating patients in hospitals. They are experts in treating common conditions that require hospitalization, and are also familiar with hospital procedures, tests, and infection control standards.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics is the medical specialty that focuses on the health of children from birth to adolescence, up to the age of 18.

Critical Care

Critical care, also known as intensive care, is medical care for patients with life-threatening illnesses or injuries.

Laboratory

A laboratory, or lab, is a place for scientific research, experimentation, and analysis.
